Study Tips

Don't like the grade you got? Well here are some things you can do to improve:

  • Take good notes. Don't take pictures of the board, instead write down what's on the board in a notebook or even on your laptop. Research has shown that people remember things better when they write it down.
  • Staying organized helps prevent any potential problems that may occur. Make a schedule to help manage your time between doing schoolwork and other commitments.
  • Remove any distractions while studying! This means turn off all your devices, whether that is your phone, television, or gaming consoles.
  • Don't cram all your studying right before an exam.
  • It's also important not to overstudy. Take breaks if you need to, and make sure you are well rested before an exam.
